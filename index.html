Our promise to you

This is a safe space

What we do

Read every submission. Cluster themes. Bring the honest picture to every Town Hall. Respond in the open, not the shadows.

What we don't do

Track who submitted what. Log IP addresses. Attempt to guess identities. Share submissions outside the leadership review circle.

What we ask of you

Be honest. Be kind. Anonymity isn't a shield for cruelty — it's a shield for candour. Write the thing you'd want said back to you.

Why this exists

Because the best ideas often come from voices that don't feel loud enough in the room. This forum turns the volume up.
